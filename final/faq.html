Q. Are our commissions free?

A. We do not offer free commissions. However, all commissions require a one time fee and pricing can vary depending on the consumer’s needs.

Q. Will the artist have to be paid royalty fees when the logo is used?

A. No, all you have to do is pay the one time fee and full ownership becomes yours.

Q. Where can we commission a logo?

A. You can contact us via our main site, or you can follow us and contact us through social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter(‘X’), or Instagram.

Q. What if we are unsatisfied with the logo created?

A. If you are unsatisfied with the product then you can request a full refund, ask the artist to edit the design, or have the artist redo the logo until you are satisfied.

Q. How much does it cost to commission a logo?

A. As mentioned before, pricing varies depending on the needs of the consumer. However, most logos typically have a starting price of $50-$200.
