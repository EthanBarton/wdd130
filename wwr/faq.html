Frequently asked questions (F.A.Q.)

One of your questions still unanswered? Contact us down below.

Do I have to sign a waiver?

A waiver does need to be signed in order to be elligible for white water rafting. Individual's 18 years old or older must sign their own waivers while children under 18 have to be listed under the parent's waiver.

Is white water rafting dangerous?

Our staff always make safety a priority. Any concerns that you may have can be found listed in the waiver.

When are white water rafting activities open during the year?

White water rafting is open from March 20th - August 20th.
